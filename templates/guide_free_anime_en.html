How to Watch Anime for Free

Last updated: July 19, 2026

Not ready to commit to a monthly subscription just to watch anime? Here's how to enjoy anime legally without spending anything.

Look for the "Free" badge

Every title page on DOKOMIRU shows a badge for each streaming service: "Free," "Included in Subscription," or "Pay-per-view." Titles marked "Free" can be watched with no extra cost. Starting with those is the simplest way to save money.

Use ad-supported free plans

Services like Abema offer plans that are free in exchange for showing ads. They're less flexible than paid tiers, but many popular titles have episodes available for free this way — great if you just want to sample a few episodes first.

Make smart use of free trial periods

Many subscription services offer a free trial period for new sign-ups. If the titles you want to watch are spread across several services, staggering your trials can let you watch everything you want while minimizing what you actually pay. That said, trial terms and auto-renewal policies vary by service, so always check the official site before signing up.

Pick one service and binge its free catalog

Rather than juggling multiple services, picking the one strongest in your favorite genre and working through its free catalog tends to be the more cost-effective approach in the long run. DOKOMIRU's genre filter makes it easy to compare which service has the most titles in the genre you care about.

Streaming availability can change — check back often

Licensing terms shift over time, and a title that was free today might move behind a paywall later. For any show you're planning to watch, it's worth double-checking its DOKOMIRU page right before you sit down to watch.
