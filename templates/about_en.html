About DOKOMIRU

What is DOKOMIRU?

DOKOMIRU was built to solve one simple annoyance: having to search separately for where an anime is streaming every single time. It aggregates listings from legal streaming services only — Abema, Netflix, dアニメストア, U-NEXT, Disney+, Hulu, and DMM TV — and never links to pirated or unofficial sources.

Our Approach

Every synopsis on this site is written in our own words after reviewing the official source material, rather than being copied from any official site. Streaming availability is checked periodically against official sources and kept as up to date as possible. That said, licensing agreements can change without notice, so please always confirm availability on the official streaming service before watching.

Who Runs This Site

DOKOMIRU is an independently run site. It started as a personal fix for the hassle of tracking down where to watch a given anime, and was published in the hope that it helps others with the same problem. The site is actively maintained, with new titles and features added over time.

Found an Error?

If you spot incorrect streaming information, or would like to request a title be added, please reach out via the Contact page.
